About Hilltop Ventures

We're a small real estate rental company based in Angola, IN. We're run by a husband and wife from the NE Indiana lakes country. We have four sons ranging from 2-22 years of age who keep us busy and young at heart. We have dreamed for years about beginning a property management company and finally decided to act on that dream in May of 2017.

Our goal is to acquire property in desirable locations in Indiana and eventually in interesting and quaint locations throughout the country.
